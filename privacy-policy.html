Privacy Policy for TechLancers

Effective Date: September 8, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TechLancers, a freelance platform that connects skilled tech professionals with clients seeking specialized services. At TechLancers, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

When you register on TechLancers or use our services, we collect the following personal information:

  • For Clients: Name, email address, phone number, payment information, and project details.
  • For Freelancers: Name, email address, phone number, payment information, portfolio, resume, and work history.

2.2. Usage Data

We collect information about your interactions with our website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring site
  • Operating system

2.3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze website performance, and provide personalized content. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

We use your personal information to:

3.2. To Communicate with You

We may use your contact information to:

  • Send updates, newsletters, and promotional materials related to TechLancers
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support

3.3. To Ensure Security and Compliance

We use personal information to:

4.3. As Required by Law

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.

5. Data Security

6.2. Opt-Out
